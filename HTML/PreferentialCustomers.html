Deals For Loyal Customers

Grateful to customers who always trust and accompany Eyeonic, we would like to send you special offers to customers of Eyeonic.

  • HOW TO BECOME A EYEONIC DEEP CUSTOMER?

    For customers who shop at the store: If you have never registered information at Eyeonic, after purchasing, please contact the store staff for assistance in registering customer information.

    For customers ordering online: When you order for the first time at the Online Store of Eyeonic the system will automatically create a member customer account for you based on the phone number ordered.

  • HOW TO BECOME VIP SILVER & VIP GOLD OF EYEONIC?

    Member customers who shop at Eyeonic stores nationwide or Online Store can participate in the program "Accumulate points" with the accumulated points redeemed in each purchase:

    • Member customers after accumulating 30 points (equivalent to 3,000,000 VND in purchase turnover) will become VIP Silver .
    • Silver VIP customer after accumulating 100 points (equivalent to 10,000,000VND purchase turnover) will become VIP Gold .
  • PROMOTIONS FOR Loyal customers

    For loyal / VIP customers, you will receive special offers when buying products at Eyeonic.com and Eyeonic stores nationwide as follows:

    • Get discounts on purchases at Eyeonic (10% for VIP Silver or 15% for VIP Gold) when buying full-priced products.
    • Receive a gift code on the occasion of customer's birthday via SMS.
    • Be given priority to receive news about promotions or new products of Eyeonic.
    • To participate in promotions exclusively for VIP customers.
      • Note:
    • The discount program for members, VIP customers is applicable to all purchases at the store and through the website Eyeonic.com.
    • Eyeonic can change the terms at any time.
