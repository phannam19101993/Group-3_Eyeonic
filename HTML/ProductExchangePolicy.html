  • In what case can customers exchange products?

    • Customers are allowed to exchange for other products when receiving products that are not in accordance with the description on the website, the product is defective due to the manufacturer, not fit or simply no longer satisfied with the purchased product.

  • EYEONIC'S RETURN POLICY:

    • Customers can change to another product only 1 time and within 7 days from the time of receipt of goods. Exchange products need to meet the following conditions:

    • The product has not been used, not dirty. (When trying on footwear products, you should wear socks and test shoes on a clean floor to make sure the product does not stain.)
    • New product selected for exchange must have a value equal to or higher than the exchanged item value. In case the product selected for exchange has a lower value than the required product, the customer will not be refunded.
    • In case the product to be exchanged is booked during the promotion period, please exchange the product during the promotion period to apply the preferential price.
    • Products with incentives of 20% or more, products of the same price, accessories (glasses, handbags, belts ...), gifts in promotions are not subject to the exchange policy on demand of the customer, unless the product is defective by the manufacturer. Therefore, when receiving goods, please check the product carefully before making payment.
